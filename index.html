About Tallinn Sightseeing

Tallinn Sightseeing is a locally owned travel company offering authentic, high-quality guided tours in Estonia and across the Baltics. We focus on genuine culture and local experiences — helping visitors discover the real Estonia, Latvia, and Lithuania beyond the usual tourist routes.

Our Team

Our guides are professional, friendly, and deeply knowledgeable about history, nature, and local traditions. Each guide brings unique expertise — from culture and architecture to wildlife and gastronomy — ensuring every guest enjoys a personal and engaging experience.

Evelin Kass — Founder & Head Guide

Evelin has more than 20 years of experience in organizing tours. Her passion for Estonia’s culture and natural heritage inspired the company’s strong local focus. While Evelin is the face of the company, she leads a talented team of guides who bring the tours to life and provide the local insights guests remember.
